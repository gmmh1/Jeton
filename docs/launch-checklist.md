# Launch Checklist

## Product Readiness

- Role-based auth enabled for `admin`, `shipper`, `operator`.
- Booking flow tested end-to-end.
- Pricing formula validated against manual rate cards.
- Tracking status transitions validated.
- QR lookup tested on web and mobile.

## Ops Readiness

- Environments configured with production secrets.
- Backups and restore test completed.
- On-call alert channels configured.
- SLA dashboard for booking and tracking latency.

## Compliance Readiness

- Audit logging enabled.
- Data retention policy documented.
- Customer data encryption in transit and at rest.
- Access controls reviewed.
